About Me

Hello! I’m Dr. Matan P, a Ph.D. graduate from the College of Engineering Guindy, Anna University. My doctoral research focused on integrating Large Language Models with Neuro-Symbolic Machine Translation frameworks to build creative and emotionally aligned AI systems for low-resource languages.

My broader research interests span large language model fine-tuning, classifier-guided alignment, explainable deep learning, time-series forecasting, and data-centric machine learning systems. I focus on translating scientific ideas into scalable, reproducible, and maintainable ML pipelines that operate effectively on real-world datasets.

I have authored peer-reviewed publications in journals such as Scientific Reports and Entropy, along with multiple IEEE international conference contributions across generative AI, explainable deep learning, and applied machine learning domains. I have presented my research at international conferences abroad, and received the Best Paper Award at IEEE CINS 2025 (BITS-Dubai) for contributions in computational intelligence and applied AI systems.

Beyond research publications, I design and implement proof-of-concept systems that bridge research innovation with practical deployment. I am actively seeking research scientist and applied AI roles where I can contribute to feasibility-driven experimentation, scalable ML system development, and impactful data-driven solutions.
